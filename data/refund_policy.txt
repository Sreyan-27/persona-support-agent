TECHCORP CLOUD - REFUND POLICY
================================

GENERAL POLICY
TechCorp Cloud offers refunds under the specific conditions outlined below. All refund requests are handled by a human billing specialist and cannot be processed through self-service, regardless of eligibility.

ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND
- Annual plans canceled within 14 days of the initial purchase: full refund.
- Confirmed duplicate charges (the same invoice charged twice due to a system error): full refund of the duplicate amount.
- Service outages exceeding the SLA threshold for Enterprise customers: prorated credit per the SLA policy (see uptime_and_sla_policy.md).
- Accidental purchase of an add-on within 48 hours, unused: full refund.

NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND
- Monthly subscription charges after the billing date, including partial-month cancellations.
- Annual plans canceled after the 14-day window.
- Charges resulting from a customer's own failure to cancel before a renewal date.
- Usage-based overage charges (API calls, storage) that were actually consumed.

PROCESSING TIME
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on the card issuer or bank.

HOW TO REQUEST
Refund requests cannot be self-served and always require a human billing specialist. Any conversation involving a refund, dispute, or "chargeback" must be escalated immediately with full transaction details (invoice number, amount, date) included in the handoff.
