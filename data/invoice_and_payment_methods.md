# Invoices and Payment Methods

## Supported Payment Methods
- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- ACH bank transfer (US-only, Enterprise plans only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans only, annual billing required)
- PayPal (monthly plans only, not available for annual billing)

## Updating a Payment Method
1. Go to Account Settings → Billing → Payment Methods.
2. Click "Add Payment Method" and enter the new card or bank details.
3. Set the new method as default, or remove the old one.
4. Changes take effect on the next scheduled billing date. To apply immediately to an overdue balance, click "Retry Charge Now" after updating.

## Viewing and Downloading Invoices
All invoices are available under Account Settings → Billing → Invoice History as downloadable PDFs. Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number and date
- Billing period covered
- Itemized charges (subscription, add-ons, usage overages)
- Tax breakdown
- Payment method used (last 4 digits only)

## Updating Billing Contact / Company Details
Billing email, company name, and tax ID can be updated under Account Settings → Billing → Company Details. Changes apply to all future invoices; past invoices are not retroactively regenerated.

## Wire Transfer Details
Enterprise customers requesting wire transfer must contact their account manager to receive banking details and a pro-forma invoice before the billing period begins. Wire transfers typically take 3–5 business days to clear, so we recommend initiating payment at least 7 days before the renewal date to avoid a grace-period downgrade.
